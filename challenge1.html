2017 South East Asian Games

The 2017 Southeast Asian Games(Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara 2017), officially known as the 29th Southeast Asian Games (or simply 29th SEA Games; Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara ke-29 or Sukan SEA ke-29) and commonly known as Kuala Lumpur 2017, was a outheast Asian multi-sport event that took place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

The closing ceremony coincided with the eve of the Malaysia's 60th Independence Day celebrations.
